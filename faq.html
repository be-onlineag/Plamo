FAQ

Answers to common questions about Row Runner and Subway Princess Runner.

Is it mobile-friendly?

Yes. The game works with swipes and taps, and the layout is responsive for smooth play on phones.

How are game results saved?

High scores and leaderboard entries are stored directly in your browser using localStorage.

Can I reset the leaderboard?

Yes—open your browser console and run localStorage.removeItem('sl_leaderboard') to clear your stats.

What controls are supported?

Swipe left/right to change lanes, swipe up to jump, and swipe down to slide. Use 'R' to restart the run at any time.

Can I customize the look?

Absolutely—adjust CSS variables in assets/css/style.css and tweak HTML text for your own theme.
